How THB helps you
find halal products.

We explained the whole process in three simple steps. Scroll down to see more.

01

Search

Is that halal?
we read the descriptions.

We search the Internet for halal products and add them to our directory for you. Whether it be a popular online marketplace such as Amazon and Walmart or a specific brand such as Nike, we have them covered.

Our team picks products in certain categories for you to browse them in one place.

02

Explore

The options are there
for you to pick.

Now, it's your turn to pick the product that is right for you.

You can browse different categories on THB from groceries to personal care to find the product you are looking for. Since we do not sell these products directly, we cannot guarantee the halal status. So, please, check with the seller to make sure the product you are buying is compliant with your values.

03

Purchase

Click the link
to make a purchase

We do not process payments on THB. When you click the link that says "BUY", you are prompted to the actual website where the product is being sold. This could be an online marketplace such as Amazon, Walmart and Etsy or a brand website such as Nike. You can use any payment method offered by the seller to finalize the purchase.

Please, note that we earn a small commission when you make a purchase using the link provided by THB.

Terms for trust and safety

Data Protection

We do not collect any personal or payment data from the visitors. We also do not process any payments on the website. THB is only an affiliate marketing website, where you can search for products to buy on third-party marketplaces and brands.

Secure Website

THB has an SSL certificate, so uses HTTPS, which is the secured version of HTTP protocol. SSL certificate helps to secure communication between the web browser and the website by encrypting messages that are sent.

Reputable Sellers

We work only with reputable brands such as Amazon, Walmart, Etsy and more. Your trust is important for us. Therefore, we do not include any product from a website that is known for fraudulent transactions.
